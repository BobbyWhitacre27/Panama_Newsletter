From its gastronomy and art, to its food and indigenous communities, Panama is its people. It is a mosaic of cultural diversity, where indigenous roots meet African heritage and Spanish influence.

Explore Panama’s vibrant cultural heritage through interactive experiences – learn how to cook traditional dishes, wander through historical towns, dance the night away under the stars to the beat of traditional music, or connect with local and indigenous communities.

Panama is home to seven thriving indigenous communities, two of which can be visited – the Guna and the Emberá – where travelers can learn about their fascinating cosmogenic beliefs and well-preserved traditions.

Cites like Portobelo are an immersive journey into Panama's Congo culture, and an opportunity to savor the delicious cuisine of the Afro-Caribbean community.

In the central provinces of Los Santos and Herrera, visitors can immerse into traditional folklore and customs including the pollera, cutarras, and diablicos.


The Cultural Heritage Tour is one of the three heritage tours we are currently offering, which gives visitors the chance to explore Panama’s culture and connect with its people. Contact us to learn more.
